My Contributions

  • I implemented:
    • Building System -
      • Building unlock and upgrade system with cash. Only the restaurants buildings can be upgraded to increase seating area. Player and npc upgrade building will unlock automatically.
      • Some buildings have special behaviors such as wool cutting simulation or breeding simulation.
      • Restaurants have a service npc inside that will serve the produced foods. Each ingrident dropped into the building will produce a specific number of food.
      • Each building has different production time and will produce specific items or cash when specific item or sheep is given.
    • AI System -
      • All the AI were created using a custom Finite State Machine System.
      • All the AI have a navmesh agent component with different settings each. The navmesh surface for each AI is different for better control.
      • Sheep AI:
        • The sheeps have Idle, Wandering, Fleeing, and Following states. The animations will change according to the states.
        • The sheeps can be captured by the player or the helper npc. The sheeps only flee from the player to make the game less chaotic.
        • While Idle or Wandering the sheeps will run towards the opposite direction of the player.
        • Baby sheeps, wooless sheeps cannnot be captured and that behavior is handled using booleans. Sheeps that are captured and are in the following state cannot be captured either.
      • Helper Npc:
        • Helper npcs are hireable from the player upgrade building. They will help the player manage the farm and gather more money.
        • Helper npcs have Idle, Wandering, Going towards sheep, Going towards building, Collecting, Going to delivery building, and Dropping states.
        • The npcs have 2 major behaviors, catching and dropping sheeps and collecting and dropping items.
        • Collecting and dropping items has the most priority.
        • During the wandering state the npc will check if there are any buildings that has produced items and pick one randomly (This was handled using a list inside the world manager. everytime a building produced something it will go into the list and once everything is collected it will come out of the list).
        • If there are no pickable items the npc will start chasing after the sheeps.
        • The collection of items and sheeps were handled to prioritize the player to handle errors.
        • If the items or sheep is already taken, the npc will go to the wandering state again and look for the next task to do.
