  • I implemented:
    • Core Gameplay Systems:-
      • Player Behavior-
        • Joystick based top-down all directional movement system.
        • Player can interact with the world using different triggers and colliders.
        • Player can pick up items or sheeps, drop items or sheeps, and interact with buildings. Made a custom bezier curve system for the collection and dropping arc.
        • Player has a limited but upgradable number of stack capacity and movement speed.
        • The main goal for the player is to grow his sheep farm.
      • Building System-
        • Building unlock and upgrade system with cash. Only the restaurants buildings can be upgraded to increase seating area. Player and npc upgrade building will unlock automatically.
        • Some buildings have special behaviors such as wool cutting simulation or breeding simulation.
        • Restaurants have a service npc inside that will serve the produced foods. Each ingrident dropped into the building will produce a specific number of food.
        • Each building has different production time and will produce specific items or cash when specific item or sheep is given.
        • There is an upgrade buiding for the player to upgrade character stats, hire npc helpers and upgrade their stats too.
      • Mission/Quest System-
        • The mission system is there to guide the players and give them clear goals.
        • Each mission gives some rewards to the player and completing one or more missions will make the player be able to unlock the next building or next task.
        • Daily and side missions where planned but were never executed further.
    • AI Systems:-
      • All the AI were created using a custom Finite State Machine System.
      • All the AI have a navmesh agent component with different settings each. The navmesh surface for each AI is different for better control.
      • Sheep Npc-
        • The sheeps have Idle, Wandering, Fleeing, and Following states. The animations will change according to the states.
        • The sheeps can be captured by the player or the helper npc. The sheeps only flee from the player to make the game less chaotic.
        • While Idle or Wandering the sheeps will run towards the opposite direction of the player.
        • Baby sheeps, wooless sheeps cannnot be captured and that behavior is handled using booleans. Sheeps that are captured and are in the following state cannot be captured either.
      • Helper Npc-
        • Helper npcs are hireable from the player upgrade building. They will help the player manage the farm and gather more money.
        • Helper npcs have Idle, Wandering, Going towards sheep, Going towards building, Collecting, Going to delivery building, and Dropping states.
        • The npcs have 2 major behaviors, catching and dropping sheeps and collecting and dropping items.
        • Collecting and dropping items has the most priority.
        • During the wandering state the npc will check if there are any buildings that has produced items and pick one randomly (This was handled using a list inside the world manager. everytime a building produced something it will go into the list and once everything is collected it will come out of the list).
        • If there are no pickable items the npc will start chasing after the sheeps.
        • The collection of items and sheeps were handled to prioritize the player to handle errors.
        • If the items or sheep is already taken, the npc will go to the wandering state again and look for the next task to do.
        • Helper npcs have limited but upgradable number of speed and stack capacity and can be hired a limited number.
      • Customer Npc-
        • The customers (both humans and the truck) have Idle, Wandering, Going towards shop, Waiting to Collect, Collecting, Moving to despawn area.
        • The customers of the Burger Joint and Icecream Parlour have an extra state for sitting and eating
        • There are some special wandering customers for the village market that doesn't do any activity other than admiring. This is done for more emmersion.
        • There is a spawning system for the customers where each shop can hold a limited number of queue.
        • At the begining of a customer life cycle, they will choose what to order based on available shops. Each customer may order multiple items and move to multiple shops.
        • Only the restaurant customers had single orders.
        • The truck also had multiple orders and it took delivery from a specific shop only.
    • UI Systems:-
      • Popups-
        • All sorts of popups including settings, upgrade and hire popup, ads or IAP popup etc.
      • World UI-
        • Unlock, Upgrade, Collect and, Drop area UIs for player interactions.
    • Monetization Systems:-
      • Ads-
        • Banner, Interstital and, Rewarded ads were implemented using the google admob.
        • There were some Rewarded video items placed on the playing field to give extra boost to the players. The rewards include extra sheep, cash, items and increased player speed, capacity and the production time of the buildings.
        • The ads were placed in a way that it doesn't disturb the player experience.
      • In-App Purchases-
        • Only the ads removal IAP was implemented.
    • Level Design:-
      • The levels are divided into 3 different areas. Each area has a somewhat different theme and different buildings.
      • The main area was the ship farm. The second area was a festival ground for selling all items while the third area was a restaurant area.
      • There were some outside blocked area as well for visuals.
      • The buildings and props were placed in each of the area manually.
    • Misc:-
      • Custom water shader using the Shader Graph for the entire map.
      • Custom behind the wall glow shader using the Shader Graph for better visuals.
      • Lighting and camera controls.
      • Particles and FX.
